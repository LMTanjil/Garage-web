1. Can I Repair My Garage Door Spring Without Calling Anyone?
No, unless you're a certified garage door technician, it is highly not recommended to replace a broken spring yourself. Replacing a garage door spring is a very dangerous task that requires precision and specific knowledge. Springs are under extreme tension, and improper handling can lead to serious injuries or even damage to your property. Additionally, installing springs incorrectly can cause further issues with your garage door system. It's essential to call a professional technician to ensure safe and accurate installation.
